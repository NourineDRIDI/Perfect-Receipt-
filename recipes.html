Mixed Greens with Sun-Dried Tomato Dressing

Prep time: 5 mins | Cook time: 15 mins | Serving: 4 people

Ingredients:








Instructions:

1 Soak the sun-dried tomatoes in soy milk for an hour.

2 Chop fresh herbs, tomato, and onions and toss them with the salad greens.

3 Grind the soaked sun-dried tomatoes with additional veggies as desired.

4 Add lemon zest to enhance flavor.

5 Combine all ingredients in a high-powered blender until they're smooth and creamy.

Instructions:

1 Soak the sun-dried tomatoes in soy milk for an hour.

2 Chop fresh herbs, tomato, and onions and toss them with the salad greens.

3 Grind the soaked sun-dried tomatoes with additional veggies as desired.

4 Add lemon zest to enhance flavor.

5 Combine all ingredients in a high-powered blender until they're smooth and creamy.

Note:

Ensure the freshness of ingredients without signs of wilting or browning.
